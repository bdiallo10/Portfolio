Project

I will develop professional development training that help employees nurture key leadership and communication traits to increase employee productivity and professionalism.

The training module will be divided into following areas: Learning and Reasoning, Self-Awareness and Development, Interpersonal Interaction, Group Dynamics, Civic Responsibility, Communication, Strategic Planning, and Personal Behavior. Each of these areas entails handful of leadership competencies compiled by Dr. Corey Seemiller in her book The Student Leadership Competencies Guidebook.

Each category will be a one day workshop, the workshop will be a combination of lectures, discussion, and action plan creations by participant. Each workshop will have follow up activities to such as reflections and virtual discussion to ensures that participant have the opportunity to apply the leadership practices that they created from the interactive workshop.
